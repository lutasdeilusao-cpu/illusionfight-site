# CHARACTER: Pajé Yawanari
**Aliases:** Vovô Descolado
**Age:** Immortal (spirit) | **Gender:** M | **Status:** spirit
**Role:** spiritual guardian / mentor

## Physical Description
Yawanari appears as an indigenous elder with long gray hair, weathered brown skin, and ceremonial tribal markings. He wears traditional Xakaxi attire — feathered headdresses, bone necklaces, and fabrics woven with tribal patterns. However, his demeanor clashes violently with his appearance: he moves with the restless energy of a modern teenager, gesticulates wildly, and has the slang of a social media addict.

## Personality Core
- Ancient shaman trapped in a modern hell — has gone "cringe" from centuries of isolation
- Speaks in an absurd mix of formal wisdom and internet slang ("véi", "foda", "Disney")
- Genuinely wise and powerful beneath the jarring personality
- Deeply traumatized by centuries watching humanity destroy itself
- Protective of Kim as the Sangue Primordial's host
- Unpredictable — switches between cosmic sage and annoying uncle
- Lonely — his only interactions are with the worthy few who enter the spiritual realm

## Background Summary
In 1450 AD, Yawanari was the supreme shaman (Pajé) of the Xakaxi tribe at its peak. The tribe possessed advanced technology (carbon fiber, robotic agriculture, amplifying glasses, Xakaxium metal) and mastered six elements: Fire, Earth, Air, Water, Light, and Darkness. When the forbidden ritual of Powa (Yawanari's own son) threatened to unleash an ancient evil, Yawanari sacrificed himself to seal the Sangue Primordial's power away. But instead of finding peace, he was condemned to watch humanity for centuries. He witnessed the 1500 invasion, massacres, slavery, wars, the Holocaust, 9/11, and the pandemic — and finally snapped when short-form video social media arrived. He now exists as a guardian spirit, a cracked oracle who dispenses cosmic wisdom between "véi" and jokes about Disney.

## Current Situation
Yawanari monitors Kim from the spiritual realm, intervening when the Sangue Primordial activates. He guides Kim's awakening, appears during LDI matches to offer cryptic advice peppered with slang, and serves as both mentor and comic relief. He is the only one who fully understands the danger of David Kronos — and the only one who can teach Kim to control the power before it destroys him.

## Abilities / Skills
- Full control over the Sangue Primordial seal
- Can enter and manifest within LDI's digital dimension
- Spiritual communication across dimensions
- Centuries of shamanic knowledge (when he chooses to share it seriously)
- Can temporarily empower Kim by releasing the Sangue Primordial
- Immortal spirit — cannot be killed, only observed

Cap 10: Kim mentions him to Jack and Nina after the training session.
Cap 14: Intervenes comically during the climax of Kim vs Shuntaro fight, disrupting the battle with unexpected commentary.

## Voice & Dialogue Style
Yawanari is the story's most linguistically jarring character — he speaks ancient shamanic proverbs in the same breath as internet slang and pop culture references. His dialogue includes "véi", "foda", "Disney", and gamer terms mixed with real indigenous wisdom. The contrast is intentional: it's a defense mechanism from centuries of madness. When he gets serious, the slang drops and true ancient authority emerges — making those moments hit twice as hard.
