# CHARACTER: Kim (Joaquim Leal Reis)
**Aliases:** Briguento 7856#$76, Kim Primordial
**Age:** 17 | **Gender:** M | **Status:** alive
**Role:** protagonist

## Physical Description
Kim has straight black hair inherited from his Xakaxi ancestors, falling messily to his shoulders. His skin is a warm brown tone with indigenous features — a naturally serious expression that strangers mistake for perpetual anger. He dresses simply: band t-shirts, worn-out jeans, always with a cap and a loose social shirt over it. His body is lean and athletic, sculpted by years of survival, street fights, and physical labor.

## Personality Core
- Extremely resilient — carries adult responsibilities since age 12 without breaking
- Sharp, sarcastic humor used as both weapon and shield
- Deeply loyal to those he trusts (Jack, later Nina, even Helena)
- Intelligent and quick-thinking in both combat and conversation
- Emotionally guarded but craves genuine connection
- Natural leader who inspires without trying — others gravitate toward his strength
- Carries a quiet sadness beneath the bravado, rooted in his abandonment trauma

## Background Summary
Kim was abandoned as a newborn and placed with Helena, a 12-year-old kidnapping victim at the time, who saved him from being abused. Helena raised him through prostitution (ages 12-18) and later through low-wage jobs, though their relationship became toxic as she descended into alcoholism. At 12, Helena declared Kim would have to support himself and "pay her back" until 18. He became a street vendor selling candy on buses, eventually gaining admission to Elite Academy through Osvaldo's help — a prestigious school where he studies on a 50% scholarship alongside Jack. Unknown to him, he carries the Sangue Primordial (Primordial Blood), the sealed power of the Xakaxi tribe, making him the key to an ancient conflict.

## Current Situation
Kim has gone viral after his brutal victory over Thunderbolt in LDI, rising to rank 1,201 worldwide. He's now being hunted by David Kronos, an ancient corrupted spirit who sees Kim as the perfect vessel (Corpo Primordial). Between balancing work, school, training in LDI, and navigating his newly complicated relationship with Helena, Kim must prepare for the fight against Shuntaro — and the greater war he doesn't yet understand.

## Abilities / Skills
- Elite hand-to-hand combat — undefeated alongside Jack in street fights
- Sangue Primordial — manifests as black flames, white hair, tribal tattoos (unstable, not yet controllable)
- Xakixi — twin hatchets that combine into a double-bladed axe connected by steel cable
- Elemental fire affinity in LDI, with hidden darkness element
- Natural charismatic salesmanship and people-reading
- High pain tolerance and combat resilience
- Mental fortitude — refuses to beg or break even under extreme torture

## Narrative Arc
Cap 1: Introduced in his harsh daily reality — conflict with Helena, selling candy on buses, arriving at Elite Academy with Jack, meeting Nina.
Cap 2: Confronts Brock's crew alongside Jack, accepts Thunderbolt's LDI challenge for money.
Cap 3: Brutally tortured and nearly killed in his first LDI match — refuses to beg, resists to the point of unconsciousness.
Cap 4: Meets Pajé Yawanari in a vision, receives Sangue Primordial, transforms into Kim Primordial and defeats Thunderbolt savagely. Becomes global viral sensation.
Cap 5: Fights and defeats Nina in a no-rules bout, earning her respect and friendship.
Cap 6: Defends a bullied student with Jack and Nina, learns the full LDI monetary system.
Cap 7: Forces Helena to reveal the truth about his father — learns he was abandoned and Helena is not his biological mother.
Cap 8: Receives a limited edition SBI from Ryan, discovers his LDI fame, begins taking the game seriously.
Cap 9: Trains in LDI with Jack and Nina, spontaneously manifests Xakixi weapons, has another vision of Pajé.
Cap 10: Learns the mechanics of mental energy and power manifestation in LDI.
Cap 11: Ambushed by Roxy and 22 thugs, leads strategic retreat to the park, three-way fight begins.
Cap 12: Defeats Roxy in brutal one-on-one combat, shows restraint and lethal precision.
Cap 13: Works security at nightclub with Jack, comes home to a transformed Helena, accepts Shuntaro's challenge.
Cap 14: Fights Shuntaro at full intensity, nearly defeats him but Rei Xamã turns the tide — chapter ends on cliffhanger with Pajé's unexpected intervention.

## Voice & Dialogue Style
Kim speaks with a mix of street-wise sarcasm and intellectual sharpness. He uses informal Portuguese ("véi", "mano", "po**") but code-switches when necessary. His humor is dry and self-deprecating. In serious moments, his speech becomes precise and cutting. He avoids sentimental declarations but shows care through action and protective behavior. His internal monologue is more vulnerable than his external speech.
