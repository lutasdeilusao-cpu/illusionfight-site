# CHARACTER: Jack
**Aliases:** —
**Age:** 17 | **Gender:** M | **Status:** alive
**Role:** supporting (best friend)

## Physical Description
Jack stands around 1.70m with a lean, athletic build. His most striking feature is his green-dyed mohawk dreadlocks, which catch light like emerald threads. Dark brown skin, confident posture, always dressed in a military-green jacket over white tees, dark worn jeans — a deliberate 90s aesthetic even in 2030. His face carries an easy smirk that makes him look perpetually amused.

## Personality Core
- Quick-witted and constantly joking — uses humor to defuse tension
- Fiercely protective of Kim, treating him like a brother
- Fearless in combat but smart enough to negotiate first
- Natural charmer with an inflated sense of his own seductive abilities
- Entrepreneurial spirit — always scheming to make money
- Surprisingly diplomatic — handles situations Kim would rather punch his way through
- Has a darker, more intimidating side that emerges when pushed too far

## Background Summary
Jack grew up in the same rough neighborhood as Kim, forging a brotherhood through shared poverty and countless street fights. The two are inseparable — when Osvaldo secured Kim's spot at Elite Academy, Kim refused to go unless Jack came too. Jack is a talented drummer and insists he and Kim have a "band" (Kim vehemently disagrees). He's been in LDI longer than Kim, ranking 10,776th worldwide, and understands the game's mechanics intimately. He works occasional security gigs with Kim and has a talent for talking his way out of (and occasionally into) trouble.

## Current Situation
Jack balances friendship, school, and work alongside Kim. He acts as Kim's guide through the LDI world, teaching him mechanics and strategy, while also managing his own rise in the rankings. After betting on Kim's first fight, Jack climbed significantly and now has real opportunities in the game. He's also developing feelings for Nina, though he handles rejection with theatrical bravado.

## Abilities / Skills
- Excellent street fighter — undefeated alongside Kim
- Strategic thinker in combat, uses environment creatively
- Skilled negotiator and peacemaker
- Drummer — strong rhythmic sense and coordination
- LDI weapon: "Vitória" (Victory) — a cane that commands stone, can fragment it into blade projectiles
- Signature move: "Vitória Finals" — launches fragmented stone blades like a machine gun
- Steampunk-style avatar in LDI

## Voice & Dialogue Style
Jack speaks with a theatrical, confident rhythm — always performing, always selling his persona. He uses playful exaggeration ("Você será o meme eterno da demonstração da falta de capacidade masculina"), invents bits on the fly, and deflects seriousness with humor. When genuinely angry, his voice drops to cold precision. He's the hype man of the duo, but also the one who knows when to talk instead of fight.
