# Lutas de Ilusão (LDI) — Monetization

## SDA — Sistema de Apostas (Betting System)

The SDA allows spectators and players to place bets on LDI battles. Bets can be placed on:
- Match winners
- Specific outcomes (first blood, method of victory, etc.)
- Ranking movements

This system creates a parallel economy where savvy spectators can profit from accurate predictions. It also incentivizes players to perform well, as their backers have financial stake in their victories.

## SDD — Sistema de Doações (Donation System)

The SDD enables fans and viewers to send direct monetary donations to players. This functions similarly to live-streaming donation platforms:
- Viewers can donate during matches or training sessions.
- High-profile players receive substantial donations from wealthy fans.
- Donations provide a direct income stream independent of match winnings.
- The system takes a percentage cut as platform fee.

## SAL — Sistema de Acesso Limitado (Limited Access System)

The SAL controls access to premium content and exclusive areas:
- **Limited Edition SBIs**: Rare hardware units sold at premium prices (60k–70k currency units).
- **Exclusive Arenas**: VIP battle zones only accessible to paying members.
- **Early Access**: Paying players get early access to new features, weapons, and elements.
- **Priority Queue**: Reduced wait times for ranked matches.
- Subscription tiers offering different levels of access.

## STE — Sistema de Treinamento Exclusivo (Exclusive Training System)

The STE provides paid training services:
- **Private Training Rooms**: Fully equipped arenas with instant recovery systems for uninterrupted practice.
- **Personal Coaches**: High-ranked players can be hired as instructors.
- **AI Opponents**: Customizable sparring bots with adjustable difficulty.
- **Technique Libraries**: Access to recorded techniques and advanced combat data.
- **Performance Analytics**: Detailed breakdown of battle statistics and improvement areas.

## SDP — Sistema de Parcerias (Partnership System)

The SDP manages sponsorships and commercial partnerships:
- **Player Sponsorships**: Brands can sponsor rising stars and top-ranked fighters.
- **Event Sponsorship**: Companies can sponsor tournaments and special events.
- **Revenue Sharing**: Partnered players share a portion of their earnings (SDA, SDD) with Yohualticit.
- **Exclusive Content Deals**: Partners get first access to promotional opportunities.
- **Cross-promotion**: Integration with external brands and platforms (e.g., PowWow social media).

## SVN — Sistema de Venda de NFTs (NFT Sale System)

The SVN handles the sale of digital collectibles through NFTs:
- **Weapon Skins**: Cosmetic variations for manifested weapons.
- **Avatar Customizations**: Unique visual modifications for player avatars.
- **Battle Highlights**: Memorable match moments tokenized as collectibles.
- **Limited Edition Items**: Rare one-of-a-kind digital assets.
- **Title/Rank Badges**: Prestigious digital markers of achievement.

Each NFT is unique and can be traded between players on the internal marketplace.

## LT Currency (Moedas)

The in-game currency used across all LDI monetization systems. Earning methods:
- Match victories in ranked battles.
- Betting wins through SDA.
- Donations received through SDD.
- Partnership payouts through SDP.
- NFT sales through SVN.

LT Currency can be used for:
- Purchasing weapon packages (random loot boxes).
- Unlocking training modules in STE.
- Buying limited access content in SAL.
- Trading in the NFT marketplace.
- Real-world exchange (subject to platform rates and limits).
