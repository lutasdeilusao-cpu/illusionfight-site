# LUTAS DE ILUSÃO — UNIVERSE PITCH

In 2030 Bravara, seventeen-year-old Kim (Joaquim Leal Reis) survives by selling candy on buses and caring for his alcoholic adoptive mother Helena. A Xakaxi descendant unaware of his ancestry, Kim's world flips when he accepts a bet from school bully Thunderbolt and enters the LDI (Lutas de Ilusão)—a hyper-realistic virtual arena where fighters wield elemental powers, feel every ounce of pain, and manifest weapons from sheer willpower. During his brutal first match, a near-death experience awakens the Sangue Primordial, an ancestral power sealed centuries ago by Pajé Yawanari. Kim transforms into Kim Primordial—white hair, tribal tattoos, black flames—and instantly rockets from obscurity to #1,201 in the global SDR ranking, becoming a viral sensation on PowWow and catching the attention of both the Yohualticit corporation and a sinister ancient spirit named David Kronos.

The LDI is more than a game—it is a fully realized transmedia ecosystem with ranking systems (SDR), monetization layers (betting, donations, NFTs, exclusive training, partnerships), and a sprawling virtual dimension (MDI) where fighters from across the globe compete for glory and real money. Kim's journey threads through his deepening friendships with Jack (his loyal, mohawked partner-in-crime) and Nina (a wealthy Muay Thai prodigy who starts as a rival), his complicated relationship with Helena, and the looming threat of David Kronos—a corrupted ancestral shaman seeking Kim's body as the Corpo Primordial to execute the Grande Purificação, a plan to destroy and renew the world. The Xakaxi tribe's lost history, from their peak in 1450 CE through the feudal invasion of 1500 CE, pulses beneath every conflict as a living legacy.

Lutas de Ilusão is a transmedia project spanning novels, digital content, and interactive storytelling. The core narrative follows Kim's rise through the LDI ranks while uncovering his Xakaxi heritage and confronting Kronos, but the universe is built to expand—through the Sagas Primordiais (prequel chronicles of the ancient Xakaxi), character-specific side stories, and the rich ecosystem of the LDI competitive scene. Themes of ancestral identity, class struggle, found family, and the elasticity of reality versus illusion drive a story that is equal parts brutal street drama, high-concept sci-fi, and spiritual epic.
