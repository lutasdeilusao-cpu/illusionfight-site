# CHAPTER 12: "Cuidado a gatinha tem garras"

**Summary:** All three fights conclude simultaneously. Nina uses Muay Thai and improvised weapons to defeat her five remaining opponents. Jack overcomes his reluctance and takes down his group. Kim dismantles Roxy's crew with brutal efficiency, then chokes Roxy unconscious. The trio reunites victorious. Jack reveals a security job at a club. Kim and Nina are left alone for the first time, and Kim becomes awkward and silent.

**Key events:**
- Nina strangles a large opponent with her legs, throws sand in eyes, uses a metal pipe
- Two of her opponents flee
- Jack disarms a knife-wielder, uses a chain against his attackers, shows reluctance fighting women
- Jack reveals a darker, intimidating side
- Kim endures Roxy's attacks without retaliating, then drops all her henchmen
- Kim strangles Roxy until she passes out
- The trio reunites with minor injuries
- Nina teases Kim; Jack joins in
- Jack reveals a security job at "Risca a Faca" — R$200 each
- Jack leaves Kim and Nina alone
- Kim becomes visibly shy and quiet around Nina

**Characters present:** Kim, Jack, Nina, Roxy, 22-24 attackers

**Tone:** Action payoff followed by awkward romance. The fights are cool, efficient, and character-revealing.

---

## BEAT 12.1 — Nina's Cunning
**Characters:** Nina, 5 remaining attackers
**Location:** Park — Left flank
**What happens:** Nina drops a massive opponent by wrapping her legs around his neck and strangling him unconscious. She throws sand in one attacker's eyes, picks up a fallen metal pipe, and uses kung-fu techniques she studied. One attacker takes a rock to the nose. Two survivors run. Nina stands among groaning bodies, breathing hard, satisfied.
**Narrative function:** Nina's fight shows tactical intelligence and formal training. She uses the environment effectively.

## BEAT 12.2 — Jack's Breaking Point
**Characters:** Jack, 7 attackers
**Location:** Park — Right flank
**What happens:** Jack disarms the knife girl smoothly. He wraps an attacker's chain around his fist and uses it as a weapon. He asks the women to stop. They charge anyway. Jack's expression shifts — the humor drains. He fights without holding back. When it's over, he stands among the fallen, shadows in his eyes. Then he smiles again, like a light switch.
**Narrative function:** Jack's dark side flickers. He's not just a joker — he's someone who's learned to smile through violence. The switch-back shows compartmentalization.

## BEAT 12.3 — Kim's Endurance
**Characters:** Kim, Roxy, 9 attackers
**Location:** Park — Front
**What happens:** Roxy hits Kim repeatedly with her spiked brass knuckles. He doesn't dodge. He takes each hit, face blank, watching her. When she's exhausted her combinations, he moves. One punch per henchman. They fall. Roxy realizes she's alone with a monster. Kim grabs her throat. She swings — he catches it. He squeezes until she goes limp. Not dead. Just done.
**Narrative function:** Kim's "show don't tell" intimidation. He absorbed damage to demonstrate his superiority. The choke is precise — he controls exactly how much force to use.

## BEAT 12.4 — Reunion
**Characters:** Kim, Jack, Nina
**Location:** Park — Central clearing
**What happens:** The three converge at the center of the park, stepping out of the treeline simultaneously. They're bruised, bloodied, grinning. Jack cracks a joke about Kim's "girlfriend." Nina teases back. For a moment, they're just kids who won a fight. But Kim's gaze lingers on Nina a beat too long.
**Narrative function:** Reunion high. The trio is proven in battle. The lingering look plants romantic subtext.

## BEAT 12.5 — The Job Offer
**Characters:** Jack, Kim, Nina
**Location:** Park exit
**What happens:** Jack reveals he found them work — security at a club called "Risca a Faca." R$200 each for the night. Kim's eyes light up — money. Jack needs to go meet the boss now. He claps Kim on the shoulder, nods to Nina, and walks away. Leaving them alone. On purpose.
**Narrative function:** Jack's intentional exit forces Kim and Nina into one-on-one. He's playing matchmaker.

## BEAT 12.6 — Awkward Silence
**Characters:** Kim, Nina
**Location:** Park exit
**What happens:** Kim and Nina stand alone. The easy banter is gone. Kim stares at his shoes. Nina waits for him to say something. He doesn't. She asks if he's okay. He says he's fine, but his voice is tight. After a painful pause, he mutters a goodbye and walks away fast. Nina watches him go, confused — and intrigued.
**Narrative function:** First solo scene between Kim and Nina. His confidence evaporates without Jack as buffer. The awkwardness signals his feelings — he's not indifferent, he's overwhelmed.
