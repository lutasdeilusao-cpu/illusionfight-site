# CHAPTER 8: "Meu bebê? Que história é essa?"

**Summary:** Helena is transformed — cooking breakfast, cleaning, calling Kim "meu bebê." Jack is confused by the change. At school, Director Walter confronts them about the fight. Ryan gives Kim a limited-edition SBI worth 60-70k. Kim discovers his fight video is top 10 worldwide on PowWow. The trio decides to start training in a private LDI room.

**Key events:**
- Helena makes breakfast and hugs Kim — Jack is baffled
- Kim tells Jack the truth about Helena on the way to school
- Jack is enraged by Helena's past
- Nina joins them at school entrance
- Director Walter threatens to revoke their scholarships over the Samuel fight
- Kim defends himself with technical arguments
- Ryan gifts Kim a limited-edition SBI
- Kim discovers his video is top 10 global on PowWow
- Kim is furious with Jack about the anonymity failure
- They decide to use a private training room in LDI

**Characters present:** Kim, Helena, Jack, Nina, Director Walter, Ryan, Elite Academy students

**Tone:** Warm-to-tense. The chapter oscillates between domestic warmth and rising pressure from Kim's growing fame.

---

## BEAT 8.1 — The Unfamiliar Morning
**Characters:** Kim, Helena, Jack
**Location:** Kim's house
**What happens:** Kim wakes to the smell of cooking. Helena is in the kitchen — dancing, making breakfast, the house clean. She hugs him, calls him "meu bebê," and wishes him a good day. Jack arrives and freezes at the door. He pulls Kim aside: "What the hell happened to your mom?" Kim just smiles.
**Narrative function:** The contrast with Chapter 1 is extreme. Shows Helena's transformation is real and immediate. Jack's confusion is comic relief.

## BEAT 8.2 — The Story Shared
**Characters:** Kim, Jack
**Location:** Street / bus
**What happens:** On the way to school, Kim tells Jack the full story — the kidnapping, the slavery, the prostitution, the non-biological revelation. Jack's humor evaporates. His fists clench. He wants to hurt someone. But there's no one to hurt. He just walks beside Kim, silent. Finally: "Your mom is a warrior, cara." Kim nods.
**Narrative function:** Jack's anger shows how deeply he cares. His reframing — "warrior" — is exactly what Kim needed to hear.

## BEAT 8.3 — The Director's Warning
**Characters:** Kim, Jack, Nina, Director Walter
**Location:** School entrance
**What happens:** Nina meets them at the gate. Director Walter intercepts them before class. He's aware of the Samuel fight. Kim argues the fight happened off school grounds, they were defending a victim, and no laws were broken. Walter is unimpressed. "One more incident, and I'll revoke your scholarships." The threat hangs in the air.
**Narrative function:** Raises the stakes. They're on probation. Fights have real consequences beyond the immediate victory.

## BEAT 8.4 — The Gift
**Characters:** Ryan, Kim, Jack, Nina
**Location:** School hallway
**What happens:** Ryan — the kid they saved — approaches them. He's grateful and hands Kim a box. Inside: a limited-edition SBI worth 60,000-70,000. Kim's hands shake. He's never held something so valuable. He tries to refuse. Ryan insists: "You saved me. This is nothing compared to that." Nina convinces Kim to keep it.
**Narrative function:** Ryan's gift is the first material reward for Kim's actions. It's also a symbol of the world he's entering — one where game equipment costs more than his entire yearly expenses.

## BEAT 8.5 — The Viral Truth
**Characters:** Kim, Jack, Nina
**Location:** School hallway
**What happens:** Nina shows Kim her phone. The video of his transformation against Thunderbolt is top 10 most-watched on PowWow — globally. Kim's stomach drops. He turns on Jack. "Você disse que eu ficaria anônimo!" Jack stammers an apology. He didn't think the video would blow up this fast. The argument is tense. Nina mediates.
**Narrative function:** Kim's anonymity is compromised. This is a major consequence — David Kronos, Shuntaro, and everyone else can now find him.

## BEAT 8.6 — The Decision
**Characters:** Kim, Jack, Nina
**Location:** School
**What happens:** After the dust settles, Kim makes a decision. He'll keep the SBI. He needs to get stronger — if he's going to be famous, he needs to be dangerous enough to survive it. Jack suggests a private training room. Nina explains how they work. They agree: training starts now.
**Narrative function:** Kim's acceptance of his new reality. He shifts from reactive to proactive. The training arc begins.
