# Xakaxi Tribe

## History (1450 CE)

The Xakaxi tribe was at its peak around **1450 CE** in what is now known as **Bravara**. They were a highly advanced indigenous civilization living in harmony with nature while possessing technology far beyond what their era would suggest. Their story is told in the **"Sagas Primordiais I: O Início!"** bonus chapter.

**Key Events:**
- The tribe lived in prosperity with advanced technology integrated into their daily lives.
- Omens appeared: a pink sky and a blood-red eclipse signaled impending catastrophe.
- **Powa**, son of Pajé Yawanari, performed a forbidden ritual, sacrificing followers to obtain **evil primordial power** (the Sangue Primordial or a corrupted version of it).
- **Pajé Yawanari** sacrificed himself to seal the primordial power, becoming a guardian spirit.
- The tribe's technology and memories were **erased** after the sealing.
- In **1500 CE**, feudal invaders arrived in Bravara and conquered the native populations.
- Yawanari, now a spirit, was condemned to watch humanity for centuries. He witnessed massacres, wars, and eventually went mad observing modern society wasting time on social media.

## Technology

The Xakaxi possessed technology that was millennia ahead of their time:

- **Carbon Fiber**: Lightweight, ultra-strong material used in construction and weaponry.
- **Robotic Agriculture**: Automated farming systems for food production.
- **Amplifier Glasses**: Optical devices that enhanced vision, likely used for hunting, warfare, and craftsmanship.
- **Xakaxium Metal**: A unique, advanced metal exclusive to the Xakaxi tribe. Its properties include exceptional durability and energy conductivity. Used in weapons and ceremonial items.
- **Nexium Metal**: A decorative metal used in Xakaxi adornments and jewelry.

## Society

### Law of Property
The tribe operated under a defined legal framework governing ownership of resources, tools, and land. The "Law of Property" structured their economic and social interactions.

### The Six Elements
The Xakaxi spiritual system was built around six fundamental elements that later became the elemental system in LDI:
1. **Fire** (Fogo)
2. **Earth** (Terra)
3. **Air** (Ar)
4. **Water** (Água)
5. **Light** (Luz)
6. **Darkness** (Escuridão)

### Social Structure
The tribe had a hierarchical yet communal structure with specialized roles:
- **Cacique (Chief)**: Supreme leader, both political and spiritual authority.
- **Matriarch/Conselheira**: Advisor and diplomatic figure.
- **Pajé (Shaman)**: Spiritual guide, keeper of knowledge, responsible for rituals.
- **Guerreiros (Warriors)**: Defenders and hunters.
- **Facções internas**: Internal political divisions existed, such as Powa's "Sangue Puro Indígena" faction.

## Key Members

### Xakaxi (Cacique)
The supreme leader of the tribe. Wise, calm, and just. Wields a ceremonial staff and commands a Lobo Guará (maned wolf) companion. He was the father of Kawa and husband of Nara.

### Nara (Matriarca)
Xakaxi's wife, a warrior, and conselheira (advisor). Diplomatic and strong-willed. Daughter of Iara, a curandeira (healer). Carries a healing amulet.

### Kawa (Young Warrior)
The heir to the tribe. Fearless and determined. Fights with a reinforced bamboo spear and leather shield.

### Powa (Antagonist)
Son of Pajé Yawanari, leader of the "Sangue Puro Indígena" faction. Motivated by vengeance. Performed a forbidden ritual, sacrificing his own followers to obtain dark power. His actions triggered the sealing of the Sangue Primordial.

### Tawira (Warrior)
Powa's right-hand man. A large, silent, loyal, and immensely strong warrior. Wields a Xakaxi war hammer.

### Pajé Yawanari (Guardian Shaman)
The supreme shaman. Sacrificed himself to seal the Sangue Primordial. Became a guardian spirit condemned to watch over humanity. Over centuries, his mind fractured. In the modern era, he manifests as "Vovô Descolado" — a cringe, modern-speaking spirit who uses slang like "véi", "foda", and references pop culture. Despite his eccentricity, he holds immense spiritual power and knowledge.

## The Sealing Ritual

The sealing of the Sangue Primordial was a catastrophic event:
- Triggered by Powa's forbidden ritual.
- Pajé Yawanari intervened, sacrificing his physical body.
- He used his spiritual essence to contain and seal the primordial power.
- The ritual erased the tribe's technological knowledge and collective memory.
- Yawanari became bound to the spiritual plane as an eternal guardian.
- The Sangue Primordial remained dormant until Kim awakened it centuries later.

## Aftermath (1500 Invasion)

In **1500 CE** (approximately 50 years after the sealing):
- Feudal invaders arrived in Bravara.
- The Xakaxi, having lost their technological knowledge and spiritual protection, were vulnerable.
- The invaders conquered the native populations.
- Yawanari was forced to watch helplessly as:
  - **1637**: Conquistadores massacred and enslaved the people.
  - **1789**: Revolta de Durkria — mass public executions.
  - **1905**: First global war.
  - **1939**: Second World War — concentration camps, bombs.
  - **2001**: Attacks on the World Trade Center.
  - **2020**: Great Void — global pandemic.
  - **2024**: Short-video social media arrives, breaking Yawanari's sanity.

Kim's indigenous ancestry traces directly back to the Xakaxi, making him the heir to their legacy — both their advanced potential and the burden of the Sangue Primordial.
