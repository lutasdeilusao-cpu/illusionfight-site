# Primordial Power (Sangue Primordial)

## What It Is

The Sangue Primordial (Primordial Blood) is an ancient ancestral power of immense strength. It manifests through black flames (chamas negras), white hair, and tribal tattoos that cover the user's body. The power is described as raw, untamed, and capable of destroying arenas with its mere shockwave. It operates beyond the normal rules of LDI — when active, it bypasses game mechanics and causes real, excessive pain that transcends the virtual simulation.

## Origin

The Sangue Primordial originates from the **Xakaxi tribe**, an advanced indigenous civilization that flourished in **Bravara around 1450 CE**. The power was so dangerous that it needed to be sealed away.

- The **Pajé Yawanari** (supreme shaman of the Xakaxi) performed a sealing ritual to contain the Sangue Primordial.
- The ritual required Yawanari's sacrifice — he gave up his physical form to become a guardian spirit.
- For centuries, the power remained dormant, sealed within the spiritual realm.
- Yawanari was condemned to observe humanity for centuries, gradually going mad as he witnessed massacres, wars, and eventually modern society's obsession with social media.

## Manifestation in Kim

Kim (Joaquim Leal Reis) is the current host of the Sangue Primordial. His descent from the Xakaxi tribe makes him a compatible vessel. The power first manifested during his battle against Thunderbolt in Chapter 4:

- In a near-death state (broken fingers, severe injuries), Kim was transported to a spiritual realm.
- He encountered Pajé Yawanari, who appeared as an eccentric, modern-speaking figure ("véi", "foda", "Disney") — a result of centuries of madness.
- Yawanari revealed knowledge of Kim's father and offered the Sangue Primordial.
- Kim accepted, and the power flooded through him across all elements (fire, water, wind, earth, light).

## Kim Primordial Transformation Details

When Kim activates the Sangue Primordial, he transforms into **Kim Primordial**:

**Visual Appearance:**
- Hair turns long and white.
- Tribal tattoos cover his body.
- Black flames (chamas negras) surround him.
- Aura of raw, destructive power.

**Combat Effects:**
- Complete instant healing of all prior injuries.
- Vastly enhanced strength, speed, and durability.
- Ability to invoke **chamas negras** (black flames) that devour opponents entirely.
- Black flames cause pain beyond normal LDI parameters — real, spiritual-level damage.
- Can destroy arenas with a single shockwave.
- Exhibits sadistic tendencies during transformation (e.g., savoring opponent's blood).

**Limitations:**
- The transformation is **temporary** — Kim cannot sustain it indefinitely.
- He cannot consciously reproduce the transformation at will initially.
- After deactivation, he returns to normal with no memory of how to reactivate it.
- The power leaves him weakened and confused afterward.

## Yohualticit Alarms

When Kim first activated the Sangue Primordial during his battle with Thunderbolt:

- **Global alarms** were triggered at Yohualticit headquarters.
- The event was detected worldwide as "Sangue Primordial" — a system anomaly.
- David Kronos, the hidden antagonist inside Yohualticit, **celebrated** the manifestation.
- Kim became an instant global phenomenon with viral videos on PowWow.
- His rank skyrocketed to **1,201** worldwide in a single battle.
- The event drew attention from top-ranked players worldwide.

## Why David Kronos Wants It

David Kronos is an ancient corrupted shaman spirit who consumes bodies to survive. His goal is the **Grande Purificação** (Great Purification) — a plan for destruction and renewal of the world.

- He currently inhabits the body of an indigenous lawyer.
- He seeks a **Corpo Primordial** (Primordial Body) — the only vessel capable of sustaining his essence.
- **Kim** is the **only** person who can serve as the Corpo Primordial.
- The Sangue Primordial makes Kim the perfect container for David Kronos's spirit.
- David Kronos has been searching for centuries for such a vessel.
- He intends to claim Kim's body to achieve the Grande Purificação.
