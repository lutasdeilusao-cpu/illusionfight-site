# Organizations

## Yohualticit

### Overview
Yohualticit is the **company that created LDI (Lutas de Ilusão)** in **2022**. The company is responsible for the SBI device, the MDI (Mundo de Ilusões), and all associated technologies. It is a globally dominant corporation in the virtual entertainment sector by 2030.

### Key Facts
- **Founded**: 2022
- **Creator of**: LDI (Lutas de Ilusão), SBI (Simulador de Batalhas de Ilusão), MDI (Mundo de Ilusões)
- **Headquarters**: Likely in Bravara (given the local setting)
- **Revenue Model**: All monetization systems — SDA, SDD, SAL, STE, SDP, SVN, and LT Currency

### David Kronos Connection
David Kronos is an ancient corrupted shaman spirit operating **inside Yohualticit**. He uses the company as a base of operations to:
- Monitor LDI activity for signs of the Sangue Primordial.
- Detect manifestations of primordial power (triggering global alarms when Kim transformed).
- Access data on all players, including their physical and psychological profiles.
- Eventually claim the Corpo Primordial (Kim's body).

The Yohualticit global alarm system was programmed to detect Sangue Primordial signatures — a feature David Kronos had embedded, unknown to most employees.

### Role in the Story
Yohualticit serves as the technological façade behind which David Kronos executes his centuries-old plan for the Grande Purificação. The company is both:
- A legitimate entertainment/technology giant providing cutting-edge virtual reality.
- A hunting ground for David Kronos to find and capture the Corpo Primordial.

The company's systems have been infiltrated/subverted by David Kronos for his own purposes. The NexusPhantasm (the LDI narrator entity) was originally created by a cult of gothic net-hackers but was officially co-opted as the MDI narrator.

### NexusPhantasm
The **NexusPhantasm** is the digital master of ceremonies for LDI matches:
- Appearance: A stylized floating skull with biomechanical tentacles.
- Origin: Created by a cult of gothic net-hackers.
- Status: Official narrator of the MDI.
- Function: Announces battles, fighters, and results with theatrical flair.

## Elite Academy

### Overview
A **futuristic school** in Bravara, representing the pinnacle of modern education in 2030. It combines advanced technology with traditional curriculum, serving students from diverse economic backgrounds through a scholarship system.

### Infrastructure
- **Classrooms**: Equipped with tablets, augmented reality systems, and virtual assistants.
- **Technology**: Cutting-edge educational technology integrated into every subject.
- **Anexo Abandonado**: An unused annex building on campus that Kim and Jack have claimed as their "headquarters."

### Students

#### Scholarship Students (50% tuition)
- **Kim (Joaquim Leal Reis)**: Protagonist, indigenous descent, demonstrates exceptional combat talent. Received his scholarship through Osvaldo's help.
- **Jack**: Kim's best friend, mohawk with green dreads, drummer, street-smart fighter.
- **Nina**: Wealthy student initially but joined Kim and Jack's circle. Muay Thai professional since age 12. Bassist.

#### Other Notable Students
- **Thunderbolt (Bruno Ribeiro)**: Wealthy, arrogant, cowardly antagonist. "O Ostentador" (The Show-off). Lost to Kim in LDI.
- **Brock**: Thunderbolt's enforcer/henchman.
- **Roxy**: Antagonist, uses soqueiras (spiked brass knuckles). Led an attack on Kim, Jack, and Nina.
- **Ryan**: Student who gave Kim a limited edition SBI as thanks for help.

### Faculty & Staff

#### Osvaldo (Gatekeeper / Mentor)
- **Role**: Porteiro (gatekeeper) of Elite Academy.
- **Background**: Former music teacher.
- **Significance**: A mentor figure to Kim and Jack. Ran a community music studio. Helped Kim secure his 50% scholarship.
- **Personality**: Kind, understanding, paternal figure.

#### Pedro Maquina (Pedro Almeida, A Máquina)
- **Role**: Mathematics and Physics teacher at Elite Academy.
- **Background**: Former olympiad medalist.
- **Personality**: Methodical, cold, fair. Respected for his precision and high standards.
- **Teaching Style**: Demanding but just, pushing students to excel.

#### Professor Romancista
- **Role**: Portuguese teacher.
- **Events**: Assigned a group project on "O Iluminismo em Bravara" (The Enlightenment in Bravara), which brought Kim, Jack, and Nina together as a team.

#### Diretor Walter
- **Role**: School director.
- **Events**: Confronted Kim, Jack, and Nina about fighting outside school. Threatened scholarship revocation.
- **Personality**: Institutional, follows rules, but not necessarily malicious.

### Scholarships
- Elite Academy offers **50% tuition scholarships** to talented low-income students.
- Selection criteria are not fully detailed but involve recommendation (Osvaldo helped Kim) and academic/behavioral standing.
- Scholarships are conditional — fighting outside school or severe misconduct can result in revocation.
- The scholarship is a critical lifeline for characters like Kim, who otherwise could not afford elite education.

### Culture & Environment
- **Mix of social classes**: Wealthy students (Thunderbolt, Nina's initial circle) coexist with scholarship students, creating social friction.
- **Music**: The school environment supports musical pursuits — Jack plays drums, Nina plays bass, and Kim is a vocalist.
- **Technology**: Advanced learning tools prepare students for a high-tech world, including LDI proficiency.
- **Discipline**: Strict behavioral codes, but enforcement depends on social status (scholarship students face higher scrutiny).
