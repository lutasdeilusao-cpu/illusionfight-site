# ISA UNIVERSE — COMPLETE TIMELINE

## Ancient History

**1450 CE** — Xakaxi tribe at peak. Highly advanced society with carbon fiber, robotic agriculture, amplifier glasses, and Xakaxium metal. Six elements mastered: Fire, Earth, Air, Water, Light, Darkness. Pajé Yawanari seals Primordial Power inside Sangue Primordial, sacrificing himself. Becomes a guardian spirit condemned to observe humanity for centuries. Goes mad watching modern humanity waste time on short-video social media.

**1500 CE** — Feudal invaders arrive in Bravara (Brazil-coded nation). Conquer defenseless natives. Xakaxi technology and memories erased from history.

## Colonial Period

**1637** — Conquistadores massacre and enslave native populations across Bravara.

**1789** — Durkria Revolt. Public mass executions follow the uprising.

## Modern History

**1905** — First Global War.

**1939** — Second Global War. Concentration camps. Atomic bombs.

**2001** — Twin towers attack in the United States.

**2020** — Grande Vazio pandemic sweeps the globe.

## The LDI Era

**2022** — Yohualticit corporation creates LDI (Lutas de Ilusão) technology. A virtual battle arena where fighters wield elemental powers, feel real pain, and manifest weapons from willpower. The SBI (Simulador de Batalhas de Ilusão) device allows neural immersion into the MDI (Mundo de Ilusões).

**2024** — Short-video social media platforms (PowWow) arrive. Pajé Yawanari, now in his "Vovô Descolado" persona, becomes chronically online. LDI and content creation explode globally.

## Present (Book 1)

**2030** — Kim's story begins. He is a 17-year-old Xakaxi descendant living in poverty with his adoptive mother Helena in Bravara. He sells candy on buses, studies at Elite Academy on a 50% scholarship arranged by gatekeeper Osvaldo, and enters LDI for the first time after a bet with Thunderbolt. His dormant Sangue Primordial awakens in Chapter 4, shooting him to #1,201 in the worldwide SDR ranking. Shuntaro Chirasu (#998 worldwide) arrives from Azuma to challenge him.
