# CHAPTER 13: "Kim mostra sua lábia com o broto!"

**Summary:** Kim goes home and shares a rare, warm evening with Helena — watching TV together for the first time in years. He and Jack work security at "Risca a Faca" where Kim handles a fight diplomatically. A quiet weekend passes. On Monday, a top-ranked LDI player named Shuntaro Chirasu (rank 998) arrives at school specifically to challenge Kim.

**Key events:**
- Kim parts awkwardly from Nina, takes a Duber home
- Helena made lunch; they watch "La Casa do Dinheiro" together
- Kim eats a home-cooked meal for the first time in 5 years
- He falls asleep satisfied
- Security job at "Risca a Faca" — Jack negotiates pay from R$200 to R$300
- Kim takes a punch to maintain peace, resolves a fight calmly
- Shuntaro Chirasu appears at school — traveled 9 hours from Azuma
- He challenges Kim to an LDI match
- Nina warns Kim about Shuntaro's skill
- Kim accepts despite the odds

**Characters present:** Kim, Helena, Jack, "Homem Grande" (club owner), Shuntaro, Nina, Elite Academy students

**Tone:** Warm and slice-of-life, then abruptly intense. The chapter is a calm before the storm.

---

## BEAT 13.1 — The Awkward Exit
**Characters:** Kim, Nina
**Location:** Street near park
**What happens:** Kim fumbles through a goodbye with Nina, hails a Duber (ride-share) and escapes. Nina watches him go, replaying the interaction. He was smooth during fights, funny with Jack, but alone with her he turned into a different person. She files this away.
**Narrative function:** Establishes their romantic dynamic — Kim is attracted but paralyzed. Nina is observant and patient.

## BEAT 13.2 — Dinner and a Show
**Characters:** Kim, Helena
**Location:** Kim's house
**What happens:** Kim walks into a transformed home. Helena cooked lunch. Real food. She suggests they watch her new show: "La Casa do Dinheiro." They sit together on the couch. For the first time in five years, Kim eats a meal his mother prepared. They laugh at the show. He falls asleep mid-episode, full and content.
**Narrative function:** Payoff for Chapter 7's revelation. Helena's transformation continues. Kim experiences a normal family moment — something he's never had.

## BEAT 13.3 — The Club
**Characters:** Kim, Jack, "Homem Grande"
**Location:** Risca a Faca nightclub
**What happens:** Kim and Jack arrive at "Risca a Faca" — a club in a dangerous part of the city. Jack negotiates with the owner, "Homem Grande," raising their pay from R$200 to R$300 each. Kim observes the clientele. This is a different world — harder, older, no rules. He feels at home.
**Narrative function:** New setting. The club represents adult responsibility and a new income stream. Jack's negotiation shows his entrepreneurial side.

## BEAT 13.4 — The Calm Bouncer
**Characters:** Kim, Jack, Club patrons
**Location:** Risca a Faca
**What happens:** A fight breaks out. Kim intervenes before it escalates. He takes a punch without retaliating — absorbs it, shows no reaction. The attacker, confused by the lack of response, backs down. Kim de-escalates, offers the man a drink on the house. Jack handles the diplomatic cleanup. "Homem Grande" nods approvingly.
**Narrative function:** Kim's maturity under pressure. Not every fight needs to be won — some just need to be ended. This is a different kind of strength.

## BEAT 13.5 — The Weekend
**Characters:** Kim, Jack, Helena
**Location:** Various
**What happens:** A quiet weekend. Kim sleeps. Eats Helena's cooking. Avoids LDI. Decompresses. Jack comes over, they play music badly, argue about the band. Helena yells at them to be quiet. It's domestic. Normal. Kim lets himself enjoy it.
**Narrative function:** Breath before the storm. The reader rests so the next chapter hits harder.

## BEAT 13.6 — The Visitor
**Characters:** Shuntaro, Kim, Jack, Nina
**Location:** Elite Academy entrance
**What happens:** Monday. A young Asian man stands at the school entrance. Traveled nine hours from Azuma. He asks for "Joaquim Leal Reis" — Kim's real name. Kim steps forward. Shuntaro introduces himself. Rank 998 globally. The "new golden boy" of LDI. He's here to challenge Kim. The air freezes.
**Narrative function:** Shuntaro's entrance is dramatic and immediate. He crossed a border to find Kim — this isn't casual. Kim's real name being known adds pressure.

## BEAT 13.7 — The Warning
**Characters:** Nina, Kim, Jack
**Location:** School entrance
**What happens:** Nina pulls Kim aside. Shuntaro isn't just high-ranked — he's known for his water-element control, his dual yo-yo weapons, and his "Rei Xamã" form. She's studied him. He's dangerous. Kim listens, then steps forward. "I accept."
**Narrative function:** Establishes Shuntaro's threat level. Kim's acceptance shows his growth — he's not just chasing money anymore, he's chasing the challenge.

## BEAT 13.8 — The Anticipation
**Characters:** Kim, Shuntaro, Jack, Nina
**Location:** School entrance
**What happens:** Shuntaro sets the terms: a ranked match, standard rules. Kim agrees. They'll meet after school in LDI. Shuntaro bows formally — a gesture Kim doesn't know how to return. He nods instead. Shuntaro departs. Kim feels the familiar hunger: the need to prove himself against someone better.
**Narrative function:** Sets up Chapter 14's fight. The formal bow contrasts Kim's street informality, highlighting the cultural gap.
