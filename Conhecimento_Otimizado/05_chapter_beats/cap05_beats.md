# CHAPTER 5: "Não me julgue"

**Summary:** Kim and Jack defeat some bullies outside school and are smoking when Nina approaches. She challenges Kim to a fight, revealing her Muay Thai expertise. Kim wins through a trick, and the three bond over the fight. Nina joins their school project group.

**Key events:**
- Kim and Jack win a post-school fight
- Nina approaches while they're smoking, demands her phone back
- Kim and Jack retrieve it with synchronized speed
- Nina challenges Kim to a no-rules fight
- Nina dominates early with Muay Thai strikes
- Kim adapts, uses "scorpion strike," then wins with a distraction (throws shoe, elbow strike)
- Jack catches Nina before she hits dangerous debris
- Nina is excited, not angry — she respects Kim's win
- They attend Portuguese class; the teacher assigns a project on Iluminismo in Bravara
- Nina joins Kim and Jack's project group

**Characters present:** Kim, Jack, Nina, Portuguese teacher ("Romancista"), Elite Academy students

**Tone:** Light, playful, action-before-romance. The fight is intense but not brutal like LDI. It's competitive joy.

---

## BEAT 5.1 — Post-Fight Calm
**Characters:** Kim, Jack
**Location:** Street near school
**What happens:** Kim and Jack have just finished a minor fight with some bullies. They're catching their breath, smoking cigarettes, laughing. Regular day. Kim is still processing his LDI transformation but pushing it down. Jack is buzzing with excitement about the PowWow video's numbers.
**Narrative function:** Transition scene. Shows their baseline state between plot events. Smoking is a recurring ritual.

## BEAT 5.2 — The Interruption
**Characters:** Nina, Kim, Jack
**Location:** Street near school
**What happens:** Nina approaches with aggressive body language. She snatches Kim's phone — a test. Kim and Jack move without words: Kim goes low, Jack goes high, and they retrieve the phone in fluid coordination. Nina's eyes widen. She saw it in the annex fight, but up close, their synchronization is something else.
**Narrative function:** Demonstrates their fight synergy and Nina's analytical mind. She's testing them, not stealing.

## BEAT 5.3 — The Challenge
**Characters:** Nina, Kim, Jack
**Location:** Empty lot / street area
**What happens:** Nina declares she wants to fight Kim. No rules. She reveals she's trained Muay Thai professionally since age 12. Kim accepts, curious. Jack steps back to referee/spectate. The two circle each other. Nina strikes first — a fast leg kick that lands solid.
**Narrative function:** Establishes Nina's combat credentials. She's not just a rich girl — she's a legitimate fighter.

## BEAT 5.4 — The Fight: Phase One
**Characters:** Kim, Nina
**Location:** Empty lot
**What happens:** Nina is faster and more technical. She lands a clean jaw shot that rocks Kim. Another to the chest drops him. Kim hits the ground, surprised. He's used to street brawling where he's the best. Nina is something else — trained, precise, relentless. Jack watches, concerned.
**Narrative function:** Humbles Kim. Shows there are different kinds of strength. Nina's technique beats his raw talent — at first.

## BEAT 5.5 — The Fight: Adaptation
**Characters:** Kim, Nina
**Location:** Empty lot
**What happens:** Kim adapts. He stops fighting her rhythm and imposes his own. He uses dirty tricks — the "scorpion strike," unpredictable angles, unorthodox movement. The fight becomes chaotic, which favors him. He takes hits but starts landing his own. The balance shifts.
**Narrative function:** Shows Kim's key combat trait: adaptation. He loses the first exchange but learns and adjusts. This is his superpower.

## BEAT 5.6 — The Distraction
**Characters:** Kim, Nina, Jack
**Location:** Empty lot
**What happens:** Kim fakes throwing his shoe — Nina flinches. In that split second, Kim closes distance and delivers a clean elbow strike that would have ended the fight. But behind Nina is debris with sharp edges. Jack darts in and catches her before she falls. The fight is over, but Nina isn't hurt. Jack's catch saves her from real injury.
**Narrative function:** The shoe throw is a classic street trick. Jack's catch shows he's watching out for Nina already. The fight ends clean — no hard feelings.

## BEAT 5.7 — Respect Earned
**Characters:** Kim, Nina, Jack
**Location:** Empty lot
**What happens:** Instead of anger, Nina is glowing. She's excited — genuinely thrilled to have found opponents who push her. She compliments their skills. The tension dissolves into mutual respect. The three share a moment of wordless understanding. They walk back to school together.
**Narrative function:** The fight was the icebreaker. Now they're equals. Nina's reaction shows she's a fighter at heart — she wanted this.

## BEAT 5.8 — The Project
**Characters:** Kim, Nina, Jack, Professor "Romancista"
**Location:** Portuguese class
**What happens:** In class, the professor "Romancista" assigns a group project on O Iluminismo in Bravara. Nina, without hesitation, says she'll work with Kim and Jack. Kim is caught off-guard. Jack grins. The three form their official alliance. The chapter closes with them as a unit.
**Narrative function:** Institutionalizes the trio. They're now a defined group. The project setup means they'll spend time together — relationship development space.
