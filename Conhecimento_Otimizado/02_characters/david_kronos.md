# CHARACTER: David Kronos
**Aliases:** —
**Age:** Immortal (ancient spirit) | **Gender:** M | **Status:** active
**Role:** main antagonist

## Physical Description
David Kronos is a corrupted ancestral spirit who consumes bodies to exist. His current host is an indigenous lawyer — a professional, composed exterior hiding a millennial darkness. He can appear as anyone, but when his power manifests, his eyes glow with an ancient, predatory light. As a spirit, he's formless darkness with echoes of shamanic adornments corrupted into something monstrous.

## Personality Core
- Millennia-old grudge — consumed by a plan for revenge and renewal
- Patient and calculating — waited centuries for the Corpo Primordial to appear
- Charismatic and intelligent — operates through his lawyer host seamlessly
- Completely amoral — sees destruction as necessary purification
- Messianic delusion — believes his "Grande Purificação" will cleanse the world
- Views other humans as tools or obstacles — no empathy remains
- Obsessed with Kim as the perfect vessel

## Background Summary
David Kronos was once a powerful Xakaxi shaman (or connected to one) who became corrupted by dark power. He was the ancient evil that Pajé Yawanari sealed away by sacrificing himself — the Sangue Primordial was the key to both sealing and accessing that power. For centuries, Kronos has sought a body strong enough to contain his essence — the "Corpo Primordial." Kim's activation of the Sangue Primordial in LDI sent alarms through Yohualticit's systems, alerting Kronos that his vessel has finally arrived. His plan: the "Grande Purificação" — total destruction and renewal of the world.

## Current Situation
Kronos is aware of Kim's existence and the manifestation of Sangue Primordial. He celebrated when it happened, knowing his millennia-long wait is nearly over. He now moves through the shadows, manipulating events to draw Kim deeper into the conflict. The Yohualticit corporation, creator of LDI, is also aware — and may be connected to Kronos's plans or actively opposing them.

## Abilities / Skills
- Complete spiritual possession of host bodies
- Millennia of accumulated knowledge and dark magic
- Controls the Sangue Primordial's source — can sense it anywhere
- Political and social manipulation through his lawyer identity
- Corrupted shamanic powers — darkness, entropy, spiritual domination
- Invulnerable without the Corpo Primordial — cannot be conventionally killed

## Narrative Arc
Cap 1-3: Unseen — referenced only through the mysterious power Kim unknowingly carries.
Cap 4: Alarms triggered worldwide when Kim activates Sangue Primordial — Kronos celebrates.
Cap 5-7: (Off-screen) Monitors Kim's progress, plans his approach.
Cap 8-10: (Off-screen) Watches Kim grow stronger, waits for the right moment.
Cap 11-13: (Off-screen) His presence looms as the stakes escalate.
Cap 14: His plan advances as Kim's power grows — the confrontation draws closer.

## Voice & Dialogue Style
David Kronos speaks with an ancient, measured cadence — formal Portuguese that betrays his centuries of existence. He uses grandiose, biblical language about purification and destiny. When addressing the current world, his tone drips with contempt for modernity. His voice is calm, never rushed, always in control. Even his threats are delivered as statements of inevitable fact.
