# CHARACTER: Nina
**Aliases:** —
**Age:** 17 | **Gender:** F | **Status:** alive
**Role:** supporting (eventual love interest / team member)

## Physical Description
Nina has vibrant pink hair cascading in silky waves to her waist, usually tied in a high ponytail. Fair skin, piercing expressive eyes, and delicate tattoos on her neck. She dresses with sophisticated elegance: designer pieces, leather accessories, smartwatch — all marking her wealthy background. Her posture is naturally regal, every movement precise from years of martial arts training.

## Personality Core
- Initially arrogant and cold — dismisses Jack immediately, keeps people at a distance
- Deep down fiercely loyal once trust is earned — transforms into a protective friend
- Competitive to her core — Muay Thai professional since 12, LDI ranking 8,724th
- Intelligent and analytical — absorbs game mechanics quickly
- Secretly lonely — wealth isolated her, and she craves genuine friendship
- Risk-taker — drawn to Kim and Jack's dangerous lifestyle
- Becomes caring and emotionally open with the group over time

## Background Summary
Nina comes from a wealthy family and attended Elite Academy as a new transfer student. She has been training Muay Thai professionally since age 12, giving her elite combat skills far beyond typical students. In LDI, she uses a gothic/punk-themed avatar and fights strategically. Initially she looked down on Kim and Jack, but after losing a no-rules fight to Kim, she gained respect and slowly integrated into their duo. She plays bass in Jack's imaginary band and genuinely enjoys the chaos of their friendship.

## Current Situation
Nina is fully part of Kim and Jack's circle, sharing their daily adventures and LDI training. She bet on Kim's first LDI fight and climbed the rankings alongside him. She's developed genuine feelings for Kim (and to a lesser extent Jack), creating tension she handles with playful provocations. She acts as the group's strategist and moral compass, though she's just as willing to throw down in a fight.

## Abilities / Skills
- Muay Thai professional since age 12 — elite striking, clinch, knees, elbows
- Skilled in multiple martial arts (kung-fu demonstrated in fights)
- Creative improvised weapon use (pipes, rocks, sand tactics)
- High-level LDI combatant (rank 8,724 worldwide)
- Analytical mind — reads opponents and situations quickly
- Plays bass guitar

## Voice & Dialogue Style
Nina speaks with cool confidence and directness — she doesn't mince words or spare feelings. Initially brutal ("Vaza!"), she softens around the group but keeps her sharp edge. She's analytical when explaining game mechanics and playful when drunk. Her provocations ("pegaria os dois") are a mix of genuine flirtation and testing boundaries. She rarely shows vulnerability in speech, but her actions reveal care.
