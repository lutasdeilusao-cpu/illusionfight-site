# CHARACTER: Shuntaro Chirasu
**Aliases:** —
**Age:** ~17-18 | **Gender:** M | **Status:** active
**Role:** rival antagonist

## Physical Description
Shuntaro is a young man of Azuma (Japanese-equivalent) origin, with a lean, athletic build. He has straight black hair, sharp Asian features, and an intense, focused gaze. His style mixes traditional and modern — he favors clean, functional clothing with nods to samurai aesthetics. In LDI, his entrance is theatrical and imposing, commanding attention.

## Personality Core
- Disciplined and dedicated — rose to rank 998 by pure skill and hard work
- Respectful toward worthy opponents, dismissive of those who don't take the game seriously
- Prideful — traveled 9 hours from Azuma specifically to challenge Kim
- Fair fighter — believes in honorable combat, not cruelty
- Confident without arrogance — knows his skill but respects his opponents' potential
- Strategic and analytical — studies opponents before engaging
- Competitive in a healthy way — seeks to grow through worthy challenges

## Background Summary
Shuntaro is a top-tier LDI player from Azuma, holding rank 998 worldwide — a position earned through dedication, not luck. He wields yo-yos with water control abilities and commands the "Rei Xamã" (King Shaman) form, one of the most powerful techniques in the game (though extremely energy-draining). He became interested in Kim after watching the viral footage of Kim's Sangue Primordial transformation. Unlike rivals driven by malice, Shuntaro is motivated by a genuine desire to test himself against the player who caused a global incident.

## Current Situation
After traveling from Azuma, Shuntaro appeared at Elite Academy asking for "Joaquim Leal Reis" by name. He formally challenged Kim to an LDI duel. The fight in chapter 14 was intense and nearly evenly matched — Kim adapted quickly and almost defeated him, but Shuntaro activated Rei Xamã at the last moment, turning the tide. The outcome remains unresolved due to Pajé Yawanari's comedic interruption.

## Abilities / Skills
- Rank 998 worldwide in LDI — elite-level competitor
- "Multiple Water Balls" — controls multiple yo-yos simultaneously with water manipulation
- "Fatal Slices" — ten serrated yo-yos deployed at once for devastating attacks
- "Rei Xamã" — ultimate form: creates a massive defensive saw on his arm; extremely powerful but limited duration
- Strategic combat mind — adapts mid-fight
- Master of distance control and zoning

## Key Relationships
Shuntaro ↔ Kim — rival (mutual respect developing)
Shuntaro ↔ High-ranked LDI players — being watched by them to evaluate Kim's power

## Narrative Arc
Cap 13: Arrives at Elite Academy from Azuma — the 9-hour journey demonstrates his seriousness. Formally challenges Kim.
Cap 14: Epic LDI battle against Kim. Phase 1: dominates Kim with water yo-yo techniques. Phase 2: Kim adapts and fights back, matching his skill. Climax: Shuntaro activates Rei Xamã, turning the fight — but the outcome is interrupted by Pajé Yawanari's unexpected appearance. (To be continued.)

## Voice & Dialogue Style
Shuntaro speaks with formality and respect, characteristic of someone raised with traditional values. He addresses opponents with courtesy even while fighting them. He's direct and honest — criticizes Kim for not taking the game seriously, praises him when he improves. His English/Portuguese is measured, occasionally formal ("Desculpe pela intrusão, mas vim de muito longe para encontrá-lo"). In combat, he's focused and minimal — his techniques speak louder than words.
