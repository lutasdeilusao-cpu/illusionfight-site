Her style balances fashion and function — she can look runway-ready while being a top 500 LDI combatant. In Bravara's fashion scene, she's recognized; in LDI, she's feared.

## Background Summary
Lisa is a top 500 ranked LDI player and a professional model from Bravara. She operates in the upper echelons of LDI's competitive scene, where she's known for her skill, fairness, and enigmatic presence. She became interested in Kim after his viral Sangue Primordial match — not just as a rival, but as someone who sensed something deeper in him. Their connection is established both as competitors and as potential romantic partners, creating a dynamic that challenges Kim's growing connection with Nina.
