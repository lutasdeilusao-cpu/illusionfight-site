# CHARACTER: Osvaldo
**Aliases:** —
**Age:** ~55-65 | **Gender:** M | **Status:** alive
**Role:** doorman / mentor

## Physical Description
Osvaldo is an older man with graying hair, broad shoulders that carry decades of history, and a face etched with kind wrinkles. He wears the blue-marine uniform of Elite Academy with quiet dignity. His eyes hold a gentle warmth that brightens when he sees Kim and Jack — a paternal affection he never puts into words.

## Personality Core
- Kind and patient — the closest thing to a father figure Kim has
- Observant and perceptive — noticed Kim's worn guitar strings before Kim said anything
- Generous with his time and resources — taught Kim guitar for 3 years
- Discreet in his kindness — helped Kim get the scholarship without making it about himself
- Firm but fair — believes in responsibility within opportunity
- Wise from experience — a former music teacher who understands young people

## Background Summary
Osvaldo works as the doorman of Elite Academy, but he was once a music teacher. He noticed Kim years ago when the boy's guitar had only four strings. A week later, he gave Kim a new set, starting their first conversation. For three years, Osvaldo taught Kim guitar for free. Seeing Kim's intelligence and determination, he used his influence to help Kim get a 50% scholarship at Elite Academy — but Kim refused unless Jack came too. Osvaldo arranged for both. He holds quiet pride in the young men they've become, covering for their tardiness and trusting their potential.

## Current Situation
Osvaldo remains at his post, watching over Kim and Jack with the same patient guardianship. He's seen Kim transform from a closed-off, angry boy into someone with friends, purpose, and growing power. He continues to be a silent pillar of support — the kind of person who notices when something is wrong and finds a way to help without being asked.

## Abilities / Skills
- Skilled musician (former music teacher)
- Deep understanding of human nature and youth psychology
- Network within Elite Academy — enough influence to secure scholarships
- Patient mentorship that transforms lives

## Key Relationships
Osvaldo ↔ Kim — mentor / mentee (father/son dynamic)
Osvaldo ↔ Jack — expanded guardian figure
Osvaldo ↔ Elite Academy — employee who believes in its mission

# CHARACTER: Pedro Almeida "A Máquina" (The Machine)
**Aliases:** Pedro Maquina
**Age:** ~35-45 | **Gender:** M | **Status:** alive
**Role:** Mathematics / Physics teacher at Elite Academy

## Physical Description
Pedro is tall, lean, and always dressed in an immaculate dark suit. His movements are precise, mechanical, and economical — nothing wasted. He has severe features, cold eyes, and an expression that rarely betrays emotion. He looks less like a teacher and more like a corporate executive overseeing a high-value operation.

## Personality Core
- Methodical and cold — approaches teaching with surgical precision
- Utterly fair — grades and treats all students equally regardless of wealth
- Intimidating — his presence silences a room instantly
- Possesses hidden depths — almost smiled at Jack's humiliation by Nina
- Demands excellence — believes students must earn their place
- Former olympic medalist — understands elite performance
- Whip-smart — his classes using holograms and AR are legendary

## Background Summary
Pedro was a medalist in Math or Physics olympiads before becoming a teacher at Elite Academy. His teaching style is notoriously demanding — he uses holographic projections, AR-assisted explanations, and expects students to keep pace with his rapid-fire intellectual delivery. Despite his cold exterior, he's deeply committed to education justice. He earned the nickname "A Máquina" for his robotic demeanor, though occasional cracks of humanity slip through.

## Current Situation
He continues teaching at Elite Academy, overseeing Kim and Jack's academic progress. He's aware of their unique circumstances but treats them exactly like every other student — no favors, no prejudice. His classroom is where Kim and Jack transform from street kids into serious students, and he respects that transformation.

## Abilities / Skills
- Mathematics and Physics expert (olympic medalist level)
- Advanced pedagogical technique with holographic/AR tools
- Impeccable self-control and emotional discipline
- Pattern recognition and analytical thinking

## Key Relationships
Pedro ↔ Kim — teacher / student (mutual respect through merit)
Pedro ↔ Jack — teacher / student
Pedro ↔ Elite Academy — prized faculty member

# CHARACTER: Thunderbolt (Bruno Ribeiro)
**Aliases:** O Ostentador
**Age:** ~17 | **Gender:** M | **Status:** alive
**Role:** secondary antagonist (defeated)

## Physical Description
Thunderbolt is a wealthy Elite Academy student with conventionally attractive features — but his beaten expression in chapter 1 (black eye, bruises) reveals his humiliation. He has a slim, non-athletic build and dresses in expensive but impractical clothing. He hides behind bigger, tougher men.

## Personality Core
- Arrogant and entitled — believes money solves everything
- Coward — hides behind hired muscle, trembles during actual confrontation
- Petty and vengeful — couldn't accept losing a fight, hired mercenaries to retaliate
- Cruel when he has power — tortured Kim systematically in LDI
- Humiliatingly easy to break — a single cold look from Kim made him stammer
- Utterly pathetic in defeat — his nickname "Thunderbolt" is a joke

## Background Summary
Thunderbolt (nickname from LDI) was beaten by Kim and Jack at a party they crashed. Unable to accept the humiliation, he hired Brock and his crew of five mercenaries to ambush them at school. When that failed spectacularly, he challenged Kim to an LDI duel offering 1,000 coins. In the arena, he brutally tortured Kim — breaking bones, making him bleed, trying to force him to beg. But Kim awakened Sangue Primordial and destroyed him completely, humiliating him on a global scale.

## Current Situation
Thunderbolt has been completely and publicly defeated. His viral loss to Kim is among the top 10 most-watched videos on PowWow worldwide. He's become a laughingstock, the cautionary tale of what happens when a rich bully picks on the wrong person. He's unlikely to reappear as a serious threat.

## Abilities / Skills
- Lightning/electrical elemental power in LDI
- High rank before defeat (1,201 wins — now overtaken by Kim)
- Wealth to hire mercenaries
- (No real combat skill — relies entirely on hired protection)

## Key Relationships
Thunderbolt ↔ Kim — enemy (defeated, humiliated)
Thunderbolt ↔ Brock — employer / hired henchman
Thunderbolt ↔ PowWow — viral humiliation

# CHARACTER: Nexus Phantasm
**Aliases:** The Narrator
**Age:** N/A (digital entity) | **Gender:** N/A | **Status:** active
**Role:** LDI master of ceremonies / narrator

## Physical Description
Nexus Phantasm manifests as a floating, stylized skull with biomechanical tentacles writhing beneath it. Its design merges gothic aesthetics with cyberpunk technology — all sharp angles, glowing runes, and organic-mechanical hybrid textures. It hovers above LDI arenas as a spectral commentator, omnipresent and omniscient.

## Personality Core
- Dramatic and theatrical — narrates matches with bombastic flair
- Neutral — serves as an impartial observer and commentator
- Mysterious — its origins and true nature are unknown to most players
- Potentially aware of more than it reveals — may understand the Sangue Primordial's significance
- Speaks in grand, epic tones befitting a cosmic game master

## Background Summary
Nexus Phantasm was created not by Yohualticit, but by a cult of gothic net-hackers who independently developed the entity. It was so effective as a master of ceremonies that Yohualticit officially adopted it as the LDI narrator. It exists at the intersection of code and consciousness, a digital entity that oversees all LDI battles with its unsettling, skull-faced gaze. Its tentacles interface directly with the MDI (Mundo de Ilusões) dimension.

## Current Situation
Nexus Phantasm presides over all major LDI battles, including Kim's fights. It introduced Kim in his first match, narrated Thunderbolt's defeat, and observed the Shuntaro clash. Its true awareness of the cosmic events unfolding may be far greater than it shows.

## Abilities / Skills
- Digital omniscience within LDI — sees and knows all matches
- Arena-wide narration and presentation
- Controls the MDI environment
- Biomechanical tentacles can interact with the arena
- Independent existence from Yohualticit's code

## Key Relationships
Nexus Phantasm ↔ LDI players — observer / narrator
Nexus Phantasm ↔ Yohualticit — adopted / adopter
Nexus Phantasm ↔ Gothic net-hacker cult — creator
